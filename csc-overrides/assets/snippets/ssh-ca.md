!!! warning "SSH certificates are required to connect to Roihu over SSH"

    To connect to Roihu, users must sign their public key to obtain a
    time-based SSH certificate. A given certificate is valid for 24 hours. Once
    it expires, a new one must be generated by signing your public key again.

    [Read the detailed instructions on signing your public key](/computing/connecting/ssh-keys.md#signing-your-public-key).
